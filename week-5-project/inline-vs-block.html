Why is this important?

Just like an article in a newspaper, how do you engage the reader? You do it by emphasising particular keywords.

Whats another use of Inline and Block elements?

Let's consider that on our webpage we want to have a paragraph to contain a link. This block element now contains an inline element, anchor tag to Google Link! that we want users to click on. This is one of the most common uses of inline elements nested inside block elements.
